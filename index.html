What is Finagic?

The only platform that combines power of AI and trading community to help you earn easily.

How does Finagic work?

  • AI scans market to find the best trading opportunities.
  • We use Artificial Intelligence to smartly analyse companies. Gone are the days where investors need to analyse financial statements, read blogs and/or articles, investment reports, company filings etc. etc. before making an investment decision.
  • We analyse it all and comprehensively give you a insights on whether you should buy or sell a company. We are the most comprehensive stock analysis platform in the market putting the power of artificial intelligence to work in the stock market

Blockchain in Finagic

  • All deals are concluded through blockchain-based smart contracts.
  • All actions are logged on blockchain and cannot be changed.
Predictive Insights And Personalized Recommendations On Stocks
Main functions of Finagic:
  • Finagic Scores : A score that takes into consideration comprehensively all the factors that affect a stock’s price - Fundamentals, Technicals, Sentiment, News to name a few. read more
  • Portfolio Management : Create and maintain a portfolio of stocks. Get predictive insights on your portfolio’s performance and risk. Automated back testing on any portfolio of stocks. read more
  • Quantitative Analysis of stocks : Automated analysis of and predictive insights on a company’s fundamentals – specifically its financials, operating metrics and cash flow. read more
  • Valuation and Pricing : Automated real time valuation of stocks using various methods. Compare this intrinsic value to market price and analyst estimates. Predict the return and risk characteristics of a stock.read more
  • Company Filings Update : analyze 10K and 10Q investor documents and get insights on a company’s progress, opportunities and/or risk factors.read more
  • Automated Technical Analysis : Automatically detect chart patterns that could move the price of a stock. Get entry and exit signals to make effective trading decisions.read more
  • Market Activity and News : Finagic automatically tracks the news and market activity and gives you insights on what could move price. Catalyst events such as liquidation’s, management sell offs, mergers etc. are automatically analyzed.read more
  • Qualitative analysis of stocks : Automated analysis of and prediction of qualitative factors such as – Industry Attractiveness, Business Fundamentals, Customer Attractiveness, Competitive Analysis, Quality of Management, Risk Factors, Management Actions, and Growth Opportunities.read more
  • Prescriptive Analytics : Get recommendations on which stocks to buy and/or sell for your portfolio. Create your own portfolio or choose from our portfolios to give you the best risk adjusted returns.read more

Do you offer a trial? : We don't. However, we have something better - a Free account that can be accessed for life. With the free plan you can start tracking your favorite stocks immediately. You can also create a single portfolio and actively manage the portfolio automatically. However the plan has limits on the number of stocks you can access and the number of portfolios you can create and track. To get the full power of Finagic and track all stocks on the US stock exchange and to actively manage your investment portfolio, we highly recommend a paid plan.
