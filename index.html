Frequently Asked Questions

How does Finagic work?

We use Artificial Intelligence to smartly analyse companies. Gone are the days where investors need to analyse financial statements, read blogs and/or articles, investment reports, company filings etc. etc. before making an investment decision. We analyse it all and comprehensively give you a insights on whether you should buy or sell a company. We are the most comprehensive stock analysis platform in the market putting the power of artificial intelligence to work in the stock market

Do you offer a trial?

We don't. However, we have something better - a Free account that can be accessed for life. With the free plan you can start tracking your favorite stocks immediately. You can also create a single portfolio and actively manage the portfolio automatically. However the plan has limits on the number of stocks you can access and the number of portfolios you can create and track. To get the full power of Finagic and track all stocks on the US stock exchange and to actively manage your investment portfolio, we highly recommend a paid plan.

Can I cancel my membership?

Of course. We would hate to see you go. However, if you must, you can cancel at any point of time from your profile.

How do I contact support?

Feel free to email us as support@finagic.com and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
