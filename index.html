Finagic Empowers Investors to Make Better Investment Decisions Using Artificial Intelligence Driven Prescriptive Analytics

At Finagic, we believe that every investor should be his or her own fund manager. Our mission is to make investing as easy as 1-2-3. Finagic is a software as a service application that gives individual investors insights and recommendations on automatically managing their investment portfolios.

Read More

Finagic Features

Here are only few of the features you will get access to when you sign up for the Finagic platform.

  • Valuation and Price: Automatically value companies in real time using various valuation models. Use artificial intelligence models to predict prices in 5 minutes, hourly, daily, weekly and monthly intervals.
  • Quantitative Analysis: Automatically analyze a company’s fundamentals such as its financials, cash flow and operating health.
  • Qualitative Analysis: Automatically analyze a company’s industry, its business fundamentals, customer attractiveness, competitors, quality of management, risk factors, management actions, and growth opportunities.
  • Sentiment Analysis: Automatically get a view on the market sentiment for a company.
  • Activity: Automatically analyze quarterly reports, annual reports and the news to get insights on the company.
  • Technical Analysis: Automatically find technical patterns to empower you to make trading decisions.
